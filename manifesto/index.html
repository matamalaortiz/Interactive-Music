Uncompleted Manifesto:

  • • Build Beautiful things. Even if nobody cares.
  • • Be diligent. You'll be spending time on each project you make. Do not waste it.
  • • All that you've done is a prototype for what is to come.
  • • Find simplicity.
  • • Learn to share your work. Be prepared to talk about it.
  • • Do side projects, spend time working on what you like.
  • • Move, experiment, try new techniques, tools and processes.
  • • Discover old and new artists at all of their forms.
  • • Review your work. Set up new challenges.
  • • Document your proccess.
  • • Never stop learning and learn to teach what you have learned.

Alejandro Matamala – 2017
